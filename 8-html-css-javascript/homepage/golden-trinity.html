The Golden Trinity

Meet the legends that redefined hypercars in the early 2010s — combining hybrid power with extreme performance and design.

Ferrari LaFerrari

The LaFerrari is Ferrari’s hybrid hypercar, blending a V12 engine with electric motors to deliver over 950 horsepower. It's the brand's most extreme road car ever made.

McLaren P1

McLaren’s first hybrid hypercar, the P1 focused on track performance and cutting-edge technology, making it one of the most agile hypercars of its time.

Porsche 918 Spyder

With a naturally aspirated V8 and two electric motors, the 918 Spyder brought precision engineering and everyday usability to the hypercar world.
